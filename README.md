## Hey, welcome!

I've spent the last 3 years in project and finance operations. Somewhere in there I realized that my favorite part of the job was never the operations — it was the moment the data started making sense.

This repo is where I explore that outside of work. Public datasets, questions I'm genuinely curious about, and notebooks where I try to figure out the answer. Nothing polished for a client. Just me poking at data that sparks curousity and drive me to see what it says.

I do have professional dashboard work — Excel, Power BI, BigQuery, the whole stack — but that lives elsewhere since it's company work. If you're curious about that side of things, reach out below and I'm happy to walk you through it.
